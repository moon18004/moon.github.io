Target Audience

Residents of the people living in the area. Also, for tourists, students, emergency services, construction workers, and relatives of residents visiting for residents.

Persona 1:

Students checking what to wear and whether to pack an umbrella.

Persona 2:

People who make sure it's a suitable day for outdoor activities.

Scenarios

1) Students going down to Utah on weekends wondered if it was safe to drive for four hours when it rained a little.

2) Driving everday for his work. He's thinking about whether to bring a jacket or not.

3) Every weekend, there is a family union. To visit family, need to know the different weather on the highway while driving.

4) One family wants to go camping on weekends and know what kind of clothes to bring.

5) The principal is wondering if the school will be canceled because of the snow.
